stablished in 2006, The Vegan Joint is a minority woman owned business.The Vegan Joint has four (4) locations in the Los Angeles and Surrounding area: The 1st and oldest location is located in the Palms area of West LA/ Culver City between Overland & Motor on National Blvd; the 2nd location in Woodland Hills on De Soto and Ventura Blvd by Target Department Store; the 3rd location is in Hollywood at the corner of Fountain and Vermont Avenue, on the second floor with a great view of Hollywood and the Griffith Observatory; and the 4th location is on 10th street right in the center of the fashion District in Downtown. All four (4) locations have full menus filled with flavors of all types, but focused on Thai vegan based foods. We are serving breakfast burritos, pancakes, and hash browns all day.
